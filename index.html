HOW TO PLAY — FART OF WAR
01

BUILD YOUR BARRACKS

Shuffle 25 Fart Character cards and deal 3 face-up to each player — those are your soldiers. Draw your hand from the Gas Station deck: 7 cards for 2 players, down to 5 for 4+.

02

DRAW, PLAY, OR BOTH

Each turn, choose: Draw & Play 1 card, Draw Twice, or Play Twice. Deploy Fart Characters, stack Modifiers on your fighters, or unleash Special Actions to throw chaos into the battlefield.

03

MODIFIER MADNESS

Each Fart Character holds up to 4 Modifier cards. Cancel out equal-opposite Modifiers to wipe them both out — a +2 Can of Beans erases a -2 Peppermint Oil and frees the slot.

04

BEWARE THE FART OF WAR

Drawing a Fart of War card ends your turn. Play a Tighty Whitey to cancel it. When the 3rd Fart of War drops uncancelled, the game ends — highest total points wins, and earns a legendary victory toot.
